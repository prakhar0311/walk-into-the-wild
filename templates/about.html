About Jungle Safari India

Who We Are

Jungle Safari India is a Wildlife Tour Company that was launched in 2015 by Abhishek Sinha and since that time it’s been a remarkable player in the tourism sector. Our company is all about bringing you closer to the wildness of India. Our company specializes in offering safari rides in some of the most famous national parks across the country.

If you’ve ever dreamed of seeing a Bengal tiger in the wild, catching a glimpse of a leopard, Sightings the King of Jungle - Lions, or simply watching Rhinos from close, we’re here to make that happen. Along with that, during the safaris, you can enjoy sightings of beautiful birds and the natural beauty of some of the most famous national parks.

Our team is made up of people who are as passionate about wildlife and nature as you are. We’ve got experienced guides, naturalists, and travel experts who know these jungles like the back of their hand. Our guides and travel experts will be there with you during the rides so that you can ask as many questions as you want. We’ve explored every park on our own and made sure that the services that we’re giving will be best for our guests.

Along with safari rides we also have the option for bookings of hotels, packages, and other things related to the wildlife sector. From thrilling jungle safari rides and memorable elephant rides to comfortable cab services and nice resort accommodations, Jungle Safari India has got it all.
